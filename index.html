Getting started

  • Set your full-day Bear Point budget in ⚙ Settings (the ☀️ value).
  • Set your low-energy day percentage there too (the ☁️ value) — e.g. 50% means half your usual budget.
  • Toggle any day between ☀️ full and ☁️ low-energy by clicking the sun or cloud icon on that day's card.
  • Each day can also have a fully custom budget — click the Bear Pts chip to type in any number.
  • The weekly total at the top reflects whichever budget each day is using.

Adding tasks

  • Click + Add task on any day to pick a chore from your library.
  • Add your own chores using + Add custom chore in the sidebar.
  • Delete a chore from the library with the × button beside it.
  • Remove a placed task by hovering over it and clicking ×.
  • Click a task's 🐻 point value to edit it directly on the card.

Splitting tasks ✂

  • If a task is too costly for one day, hover over it and click ✂.
  • The split dialog breaks it into two pieces by default — rename, adjust points, and assign each to a different day.
  • Add more pieces with + Add another piece.
  • Total points across all pieces cannot exceed the original. The bar turns green when fully assigned.
  • Split pieces appear in purple on their day cards, with a note showing the original task name.

Printing & accessibility

  • Click 🖨 Print week to print a fridge-friendly landscape summary.
  • Choose whether to include point values before printing.
  • Toggle ◑ High contrast for a black-and-white high-contrast view.
  • All features are fully keyboard and screen reader accessible.
  • Your plan saves automatically in your browser — no account needed.
